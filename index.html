Instructions

First of all, they're all the enemy. Shoot them.
Just..don't hit the ground that you're on. You need that.

Input commands:

  • Turret Controls: Right/Left
  • Power Controls: Up/Down
  • Fire: Spacebar
  • Next Ammo Type: Enter
  • Previous Ammo Type: Shift
